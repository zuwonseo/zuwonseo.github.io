San Francisco, US

Our first city is San Francisco, United States! Its coordinates are 37.5°N, 122.5°W. It is located iin Northern California on a peninsula between the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco Bay.

Santiago, Chile

Next city is Santiago, Chile. Its coordinates are 33.5°S, 70.7°W and it is located in Chile's Maipo Valley surrounded by the Andes Mountain Range, about 100 km east of the Pacific Ocean coast. Santiago has a cool semi-arid climate. With mediterranean patterns with warm dry summers and cold humid winters. It doesn't have the four seasons.On the hottest days, temperatures reach up to 35°C.

Venice, Italy

Next up is Venice, Italy, located on 45.4° N, 12.3° E. It is in northern Italy, on the northwestern part of the Adriatic Sea.

Cairo, Egypt

The third city is Cairo, Egypt, located on 30.0° N, 31.2° E. It is in northeastern Egypt, primarily on the eastern shore of the Nile River. Cairo’s climate is dry, hot, and dominated by desert. During mild winters there is rain falling along the coastal areas, and summers are extremely hot. Prevailing winds change daytime temperatures.

Seoul, South Korea

Fourth city is Seoul, South Korea. Its coordinates are 37.6° N, 126.9° E. It is in northwestern part of the country, close to the border with North Korea, and the Yellow Seashores. Seoul’s climate is overall humid but with a very dry winter. It has four seasons, and temperature changes can be extreme, with really hot summers and extremely cold winters.

Melbourne, Austrailia

Last city is Melbourne, Australia. Its coordinates are 37.8° S, 144.9° E. Its located in southeastern part of mainland Australia, within the state of Victoria.
